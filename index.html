Safety Tips

Never Share OTP

Legitimate banks or services will never ask for your One-Time Password over a phone call.

Avoid Random Links

Don't click on SMS or WhatsApp links asking to update KYC or claiming you won a lottery.

QR Code Trap

Scanning a QR code is for SENDING money, not receiving it. Never scan for receiving payments.
